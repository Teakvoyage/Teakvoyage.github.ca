ASIAN HERITAGE MONTH

WHAT IS THIS WEBSITE?

This website will cover topics relating to the Asian Heritage Month. This website will include information about Asian Heritage Month in general, organizations supporting the cause, profiles of Asian immigration in Canada, the impacts of Asian immigration, a timeline of immigration, and how you can support.

ORGANIZATIONS

These are organizations and initiatives that support Asian communities all across Canada. This webpage will help you find them to support.

PROFILES

Visit profiles of key Asian immigration into Canada throughout history. This includes Chinese, Japanese, South Asian, and Vietnamese immigration.

IMPACT

Take a look at the economic, cultural, challenges and achievements of Asian immigrants in Canada. This includes contributions to Canada's workforce, traditions, and the discrimination they faced along the way.

TIMELINE

See a timeline of Asian immigration waves across Canada from the early 19th century to contemporary immigration.

INVOLVE

Get involved in helping Asian Heritage. Support community engagement by sharing your story and contributing to research.
