Fun Facts

Some Fun Facts

This day is a serious topic with great causes. It is important to learn about it, so here are some fun facts about International Women's Day.

Purple

The color for International Women's Day is purple. This symbolic color stands for justice, dignity, and a great sign of unity. This color is usually accompanied by white or green. Remember to wear purple if you want to show your support, but it isn't a necessity.

Right To Vote

International Women's Day, or rather Women's Day at the time, achieved the right of women to vote in Russia in the early 1900s. In a campaign for "Bread and peace" women protested World War 1 and sought after gender equality. This campaign resulted in the right to vote for women! This shows how this day can be beneficial.

100+ Years

International Women's Day has been celebrated for over one hundred years! It originally started in America by the American Socialist Party. This day has lasted for a very long time and is still going strong. Over the years, it has been accompanied by many achievements!
