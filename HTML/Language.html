International Mother Language Day



What Is It?

International Mother Language Day is a celebration on the 21 of February this year. It acknowledges that linguistic diversity is facing threats of extinction. Many multilingual and multicultural societies require their language for their traditional knowledge and indentity. 40% of the global population is unable to access education in their mother language. International Mother Language Day promotes inclusion and acknowledgement of these problems and promotes linguistic and cultural diversity.
